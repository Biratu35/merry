Biography

I am a passionate Ethiopian actress dedicated to bringing powerful stories to life on screen. With a deep love for cinematic expression and a commitment to authentic performance, I have portrayed diverse characters across genres — from emotionally charged dramas to socially conscious narratives. I believe in the power of film to reflect our culture, inspire change, and connect audiences both locally and globally.
